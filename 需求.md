名词从句教学大纲
一、主语从句（Subject Clause）
定义
主语从句是指一个从句在句子中充当主语，表示“某件事情或某种情况”。
基本结构
（1）从句直接作主语：That / Whether / Wh- 从句 + 谓语
例：That he is honest is important. / Whether she will come is unknown. / What he said is true.
（2）形式主语结构：It + be + adj./n. + that / whether / wh- 从句
例：It is obvious that he is right. / It is unclear whether they will agree.
常见引导词
（1）that：That he failed surprised us. / It is surprising that he refused to help despite repeated requests.
（2）whether：Whether he will come is unknown. / Whether we should continue the project remains a difficult decision.
（3）连接代词：
what：What he said is true. / What he failed to explain caused confusion among the audience.
who：Who will go is not decided. / Who will take responsibility for the failure is still unclear.
whom：Whom they chose is unknown. / Whom the committee will appoint remains uncertain.
whose：Whose book was lost is unclear. / Whose proposal will be accepted is still under discussion.
which：Which one is better is unclear. / Which solution will work best depends on the available resources.
whoever：Whoever comes is welcome. / Whoever finishes the task first will be rewarded.
whomever：Whomever they choose is acceptable. / Whomever you recommend will be considered seriously.
whatever：Whatever he says is true. / Whatever he decides will affect the outcome of the project.
whichever：Whichever you choose is fine. / Whichever option you select will influence your future career path.
（4）连接副词：
when：When he will arrive is unknown. / When the meeting will begin has not been announced yet.
where：Where she lives is unknown. / Where the conference will be held remains undecided.
why：Why he left is unclear. / Why she refused the offer remains a mystery.
how：How he solved it is interesting. / How we can improve the system requires further discussion.
语法功能
1. 充当句子主语，表示一个完整事件或事实。
2. 承载句子核心信息（信息焦点）。
3. 常用于表达判断、评价、结论。
4. 常与 it 形式主语搭配，使句子结构更自然。
5. 整体视为单一单位，控制谓语单数形式。
二、宾语从句（Object Clause）
定义
宾语从句是指一个从句在句子中充当宾语，表示动作或认知的内容。
基本结构
（1）动词 + 从句：主语 + 动词 + that / whether / if / wh- 从句
例：I think that he is right. / I know what he wants.
（2）介词 + 从句：介词 + whether / wh- 从句
例：It depends on whether he agrees.
（3）形式宾语结构：主语 + 动词 + it + adj. + that 从句
例：I find it important that he attends.
常见引导词
（1）that：I think that he is right. / She said that she had completed the task before the deadline.
（2）whether：I don’t know whether he will come. / She is considering whether she should accept the offer.
（3）if：I wonder if he is busy. / She asked if I had submitted the report on time.
（4）连接代词：
what：I know what he wants. / I understand what he meant by his words.
who：I know who called me. / She wants to know who will attend the meeting.
whom：I know whom she invited. / The manager asked whom we had consulted before making the decision.
whose：I know whose book it is. / He asked whose responsibility it was.
which：I know which one you like. / She is deciding which option she should choose.
whoever：I will support whoever you choose. / The teacher will help whoever needs assistance.
whomever：You can invite whomever you like. / They will support whomever you recommend for the position.
whatever：Take whatever you need. / You can say whatever you want during the discussion.
whichever：Choose whichever you prefer. / You may select whichever method works best for you.
（5）连接副词：
when：I know when he will arrive. / She asked when the results would be announced.
where：I know where she lives. / He wants to know where we should meet.
why：I know why he left. / She explained why she had made that decision.
how：I know how he did it. / They discussed how they could improve the system.
语法功能
1. 充当宾语，表示“知道、说、想”的内容。
2. 承载思维内容，如 think、believe 等动词后的内容。
3. 承载言语内容，如 say、tell、explain 等动词后的内容。
4. 表达未知或疑问信息（whether / wh-）。
5. 可作介词宾语或形容词补足语。
6. 往往是句子中信息最核心的部分。
7. 受主句控制：语序用陈述句语序，时态常体现时态呼应。
三、表语从句（Predicative Clause）
定义
表语从句是指一个从句在系动词后作表语，说明主语的内容或本质。
基本结构
主语 + 系动词 + that / whether / wh- 从句
例：The problem is that we are late. / The question is whether we should go. / That is what I mean.
常见引导词
（1）that：The truth is that he lied. / The problem is that we did not prepare enough.
（2）whether：The question is whether he will come. / The issue is whether we should continue under current conditions.
（3）连接代词：
what：That is what I want. / The key point is what we should focus on.
who：The question is who will go. / The problem is who will take responsibility.
which：The question is which one is better. / The issue is which method will be more effective.
（4）连接副词：
why：This is why he left. / The reason is why he refused.
how：The problem is how we solve it. / The concern is how we can improve efficiency.
when：The question is when we should start. / The issue is when the project will be completed.
where：The problem is where we should go. / The difficulty is where we can find enough resources.
语法功能
1. 充当表语，对主语进行解释说明。
2. 表达事实、本质、定义。
3. 用于说明问题的具体内容。
4. 可表达原因或结果关系（why / how）。
5. 常用于总结、解释、推进逻辑。
6. 可实现语篇衔接，如 That is what...。

四、同位语从句（Appositive Clause）
定义
同位语从句是指一个从句放在名词后，对该名词的具体内容进行解释说明。
基本结构
名词 + that / whether / wh- 从句
例：The fact that he lied shocked us. / The idea that we should leave is reasonable.
常见引导词
（1）that：The fact that he lied shocked us. / The news that she had won spread quickly.
（2）whether：The question whether he will come remains. / The issue whether we should continue is still under discussion.
（3）连接副词：
why：The question why he left puzzled us. / The problem why the system failed needs investigation.
how：The problem how we solve it remains. / The issue how we can improve efficiency is being discussed.
语法功能
1. 解释说明前面名词的具体内容。
2. 将抽象名词具体化，如 fact、idea、news。
3. 承载信息内容或观点。
4. 可表达问题、不确定性或假设。
5. 实现信息压缩，把完整句子压缩进名词结构中。
6. 在学术写作中常用于提出观点、表达假设和进行论证。

1、系统模块，中的反思输出模块，就不要了，这个太复杂了。现在的ai，在回答完后，不是会加一小段话，说“还需要什么帮助吗？”。那我们的ai，就把这小段话，改为，问学习者的一些问题，“比如，你还能教我更多吗？之类的”。
2、关键技术难点，AI 的"受限推理"机制。打算使用system prompt对ai进行约束。同时，每个学习者都分别去教一个ai。那我在想，怎么设计ai的知识，是使用rag吗？还是给ai一个md作为记忆。
3、规则的存储与结构化，学生的输入肯定是自由文本。但是ai的记忆，该怎么存储？这个是需要考虑的。
4、学习状态可视化。这个其实很简单。第一，需要保持学习者和ai的交互记录。第二，调用一个api接口，对这个交互记录，做一下提炼也保存着就行。第三，是可以查看ai的记忆。 至于，测试的结果，感觉从逻辑上来说，和这个是稍微分开的。
5、标准化测试 vs 自由测试，这两个都要有。面向学生，学生可以输入题目或者是对话来考考AI（应该和train也就是对话，是分开的区域），学生考AI是不纳入记忆的。但是这部分对话，需要保存。面向教师，教师应该可以批量的输入测试题，然后一键对所有ai进行测试（需要绑定学生的id），这部分的测试数据，应该可以看出ai的得分，这个记录也要保留。（只要是我们的ai回答问题，一定只能从教了的知识中出发。）
6、多人/分组机制，每个人教一个自己的ai。不要分组。
7、对于，Teach（规则教学）和Train（例句训练），从逻辑上来说，是不是都一样的？如果都一样的话，那就不用新建立一个窗口了？
8、AI 的"装傻"程度：AI 是否需要在学生没教到的知识点上"真的答错"？还是说 AI 可以表现出不确定（"我不太确定，因为你还没教我这个"），但不一定要给出错误答案？，这个先说是，AI表现出不确定，也就是答错。因为我们不是有测试吗？在测试中，不确定就是答错。
